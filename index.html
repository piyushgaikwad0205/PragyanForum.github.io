About Our Department

The Department of Information Technology & CSE(Data Science) is dedicated to excellence in research, teaching, and the provision of the appropriate echo system for fostering students’ developing professional abilities. Modern laboratories and improved learning facilities are available in the department enabling students to participate in ongoing research and learning. The students receive training in industry-relevant skills that enable them to land jobs at prestigious organisations and continue to flourish throughout their professional careers.
